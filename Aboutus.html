About Shalom

Our Story

Established on June 5th, 1984

National Highway 45, Otteri, Vandalur, Chennai, Tamil Nadu 600048

Shalom Matriculation Higher Secondary School began its journey with a vision to provide quality education that nurtures not just academic excellence but also character development and holistic growth. Over the past four decades, we have grown from a small institution to one of Chennai's most respected educational establishments, touching thousands of lives and creating future leaders who contribute meaningfully to society.

Our Future Goals

Transition to CBSE Affiliation

In our continuous pursuit of educational excellence, Shalom Matriculation Higher Secondary School is preparing for a transformative upgrade to CBSE (Central Board of Secondary Education) affiliation. This strategic move will provide our students with:

  • National-level curriculum aligned with global standards
  • Enhanced opportunities for higher education across India
  • Comprehensive skill development programs
  • Advanced assessment and evaluation methods
  • Improved infrastructure and technology integration
  • Greater exposure to competitive examinations

We are investing significantly in infrastructure upgrades, teacher training, and curriculum development to ensure a smooth transition while maintaining our core values of dedication, devotion, and discipline. This transformation represents our commitment to providing world-class education that prepares students for the challenges of the 21st century while staying rooted in strong moral and ethical foundations.
