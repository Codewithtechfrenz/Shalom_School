Our Pre-Primary Philosophy

At the heart of our pre-primary program is a belief that learning should be fun, engaging, and meaningful. Through interactive play and discovery, we lay the foundations for strong early learning and child development.

By encouraging exploration and hands-on experiences, we foster holistic development and a lifelong love for learning. Every child is guided to grow confidently, socially, and academically from day one.

Program Highlights

CBSE Curriculum with Innovation

Shalom Matric Hr. Sec. School follows a CBSE-aligned curriculum enriched with design thinking and modern teaching methods. Strong foundations in English and Mathematics are built from the early years, while essential literacy and numeracy skills are developed across all subjects through engaging, activity-based learning.

All classrooms are equipped with smart boards and digital learning tools to support interactive lessons. This technology-enabled environment makes learning more engaging, effective, and enjoyable for every student.
