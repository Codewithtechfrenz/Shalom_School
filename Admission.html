Admission Process

Follow these simple steps to secure your child's admission

01

Enquiry & Form Collection

Please visit our school office to collect an admission form. Alternatively, you may request a form via email or phone. Ensure the application is completed carefully, providing accurate details regarding your child and family.

Required Information:

  • Student's personal details
  • Parent/Guardian information
  • Previous school details (if applicable)
  • Emergency contact information
02

Document Submission

Submit the completed application form along with all required documents at the school admission office. Ensure all documents are properly attested and photocopied as per requirements.

Documents Needed:

  • Birth certificate (original & copy)
  • Transfer certificate (if applicable)
  • Previous academic records
  • Passport-size photographs (4 copies)
  • Address proof
  • Caste certificate (if applicable)
03

Entrance Assessment

Students will undergo an age-appropriate assessment to evaluate their academic readiness and placement. This helps us understand the student's current level and provide appropriate support.

Assessment Areas:

  • Language skills (reading & writing)
  • Mathematical ability
  • General awareness
  • Logical reasoning (for higher classes)
04

Parent-Student Interview

A personal interaction session with the school principal and admission committee to discuss your expectations, understand our values, and ensure alignment with the school's educational philosophy.

Discussion Topics:

  • Student's interests and hobbies
  • Learning style preferences
  • Special needs or requirements
  • Parent expectations and goals
05

Admission Offer

Upon successful completion of the assessment and interview, you will receive an admission offer letter within 5-7 working days via email and physical mail.

Offer Letter Contains:

  • Admission confirmation
  • Fee structure details
  • Payment deadline
  • Joining formalities checklist
06

Fee Payment & Confirmation

Complete the admission by paying the required fees within the stipulated time. Upon payment, you will receive official confirmation and your child's student ID.

Payment Options:

  • Cash/Cheque at school office
  • Online bank transfer
  • Demand draft
  • Payment gateway (coming soon)

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common admission queries

What is the admission age criteria for different classes?

The age criteria for admission varies by grade level:

  • LKG: Minimum 3 years as of June 1st
  • UKG: Minimum 4 years as of June 1st
  • Class I: Minimum 5 years as of June 1st
  • Higher Classes: Age requirements follow state education board guidelines

Please note that age proof (birth certificate) is mandatory during admission.

When does the admission process begin each year?

Our admission process typically follows this timeline:

  • November - December: Admission notification and form distribution
  • January - February: Submission of applications and entrance tests
  • March: Interviews and admission offers
  • April - May: Fee payment and final confirmation
  • June: Academic year begins

However, we accept applications throughout the year based on seat availability in specific classes.

What is the fee structure and payment schedule?

Our fee structure is designed to be transparent and affordable:

  • Annual Tuition Fee: Payable in three installments (June, October, January)
  • Transport Fee: Optional, based on distance and route
  • Activity Fee: Covers sports, arts, and extracurricular activities

Detailed fee structure is provided in the admission form. We offer sibling discounts and merit scholarships for eligible students.

Do you provide transportation facilities?

Yes, we offer comprehensive and safe transportation services:

  • Wide Coverage: Bus routes cover major areas of Chennai including Tambaram, Chrompet, Pallavaram, and surrounding localities
  • Safety Features: GPS tracking, CCTV cameras in all buses, trained drivers and attendants
  • Regular Maintenance: All vehicles undergo periodic safety checks
  • Parent Communication: SMS alerts for pick-up and drop-off times

Transport fee is charged separately based on the distance from school. Contact our transport coordinator for specific route and fee details.

What is the student-teacher ratio and class strength?

We maintain optimal class sizes for effective learning:

  • Pre-Primary (LKG-UKG): Maximum 25 students per class with 1 teacher and 1 assistant
  • Primary (I-V): Maximum 30 students per class with dedicated subject teachers
  • Middle & Secondary (VI-XII): Maximum 35 students per class with specialized faculty

This ensures personalized attention to each student. Additionally, we provide remedial classes and extra support for students who need additional help in any subject.
