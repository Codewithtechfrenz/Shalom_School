Welcome to Shalom Matriculation Higher Secondary School


Established on June 5th, 1984

G.S.T Road, Vandalur, Chennai, Tamil Nadu 600 048

For nearly four decades, Shalom Matriculation Higher Secondary School has been a beacon of educational excellence, nurturing young minds and shaping future leaders. Our institution stands committed to providing quality education that combines academic rigor with holistic development, preparing students to excel in an ever-changing world.

Dedicated Effort

Our passionate educators go above and beyond to ensure every student receives personalized attention and guidance, fostering a love for learning that lasts a lifetime. We believe in dedication that transforms potential into excellence.

Student Safety

Safety is our top priority. With 24/7 security, CCTV surveillance, trained safety personnel, and comprehensive health protocols, we ensure a secure environment where students can focus on learning and growing with peace of mind.

Deep Knowledge

Our comprehensive curriculum combines traditional wisdom with modern pedagogy, encouraging critical thinking, creativity, and innovation. We prepare students not just for exams, but for life's challenges and opportunities.

Academic Programs

Pre-Primary

Building strong foundations through play-based learning, sensory activities, and social skill development. Our nurturing environment helps young learners discover the joy of education.

Primary

Fostering curiosity and creativity through interactive learning methods, hands-on experiences, and foundational skills in literacy, numeracy, and critical thinking.

Middle School

Developing independent learners through challenging curriculum, project-based learning, and opportunities for exploration across diverse subjects and extracurricular activities.

Secondary

Preparing students for higher education and beyond with rigorous academics, competitive exam coaching, career counseling, and leadership development opportunities.

Admission Process

1

Application Form

Fill the admission form available at the school office.

2

Submit Documents

Submit required documents including birth certificate, previous school records, and photographs.

3

Entrance Test

Appear for age-appropriate entrance assessment to evaluate readiness and placement.

4

Interview

Parent-student interaction with the admission committee to understand expectations and goals.

5

Confirmation

Receive admission confirmation and complete fee payment to secure your child's seat.

World-Class Facilities

Science Labs

State-of-the-art physics, chemistry, and biology laboratories equipped with modern apparatus for hands-on experiments.

Computer Lab

Advanced computer lab with latest systems, high-speed internet, and comprehensive software for digital learning.

Library

Extensive collection of books, journals, and digital resources fostering reading habits and research skills.

Sports Complex

Multi-purpose sports facilities including cricket, football, basketball, volleyball courts, and indoor games area.

Transportation

Safe and reliable bus service covering major routes across Chennai with GPS tracking and trained drivers.

Auditorium

Modern auditorium with excellent acoustics and seating for cultural events, assemblies, and performances.
